What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) connects devices to the web, enabling data sharing and remote control.

From smart homes to connected cars, IoT transforms everyday devices into powerful data tools.

With IoT, devices can communicate with each other and share insights, automating processes and enhancing efficiency.

In weather monitoring, IoT allows real-time data collection, providing accurate forecasts and analysis.
